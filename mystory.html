This is my developer story.

      As a child, there was no computer or even cell-phone in the family.
      In the high School, we had few computers for Computer Lab class for the whole school, and I had only two Lab classes in the the whole school year to learn how turn-on and off a PC, how to search on an Internet Explorer, how to use Microsoft word and excel, and saving files.
      In the college, I had a better access to computers at the college's library where I used to use computers to gather more information when I had assignments.
      After I had arrived in the USA, I started going to the Public library where I had more access to use the computer for an hour at a time, and there I started teaching myself the basics of a computer.
        I always had an interest and love for technology, so I bought my first Laptop called Toshiba-Satellite.
        Currently, I joined this great Refcode community in 2022, to learn more about Software Engineering where my dream is always as being a Software Enginee.
