In the high School, we had few computers for Computer Lab class for the whole school, and I had few Lab classes where I was thought just the basics or introduction to turn on and off a computer, using Internet Explorer, & some Microsoft word, excel, and saving files.

Currently, I joined Refcode Lab community in the 2022-Spring-cohort training.
